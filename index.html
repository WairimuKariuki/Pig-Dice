Here are the game rules

  1. The game has 2 players playing in rounds
  2. In each turn a player rolls a dice as many times as he can.Each result is added to their <round score
  3. If a player rolls a 1,all their round score get lost...then it's the next player turn.
  4. the player can choose to hold,which means their round score get added to the total score
  5. The first playerTO reach 100 <Wins the game
